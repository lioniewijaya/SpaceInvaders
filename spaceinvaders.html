Space Invaders

Hello there space hero! You are tasked to guard your ship from the space invaders.
Shoot all the aliens and avoid bullets to proceed to the next level!

How to Play?

Ship can only move horizontally.
- Press < (arrow right) to move to the right.
- Press > (arrow left) to move to the left.

Both aliens and ship can shoot bullets.
Bullet can destroy both ship and alien and damage shield.
A bullet cannot destroy another bullet.
- Press ^ (arrow up) to shoot.

A shield protects ship from getting destroyed.
Part of damaged shield can be passed by bullet.

If ship is destroyed, player lose and game is put on halt until player choose to restart.
Level and score are resetted.
- Press space bar to restart game after player died.

For every alien destroyed, player scores a point.
Aliens may speed up at some point when player have killed some number of aliens in the level.
If player destroyed all aliens on screen, player proceeds to the next level.
